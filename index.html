About Carib Condominiums

Carib Ocho Rios Condominiums sits directly on the Caribbean Sea with uninterrupted views of the ocean and a spectacular reef. The complex is within the city on a large piece of land, offering the perfect balance of convenience and tranquility.

This spacious 2-bedroom apartment was completely refurbished and painted in 2023. The balcony overlooks a beautiful reef, and the unit features 3 TVs for your entertainment. Enjoy free parking in our large parking lot, including a disabled parking spot close to the apartment.

Perfectly located within walking distance to all the eating and night spots in Ocho Rios, and just a short drive from Dunn's River Falls and Mystic Mountain. The apartment includes accessibility features with grab bars in both bathrooms, plus comprehensive fire safety equipment for your peace of mind.
